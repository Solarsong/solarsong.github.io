Help a pug puppy chase pesky cats from his yard!

A fast-paced arcade bark 'em up that pitches a tiny pug puppy against the all of the cats in the neighbourhood. The cats are no match for you one-on-one, but if you let too many cats into your yard, they'll take you down. Learn the strengths and weaknesses of each cat. Figure out the best way to survive the onslaught. You will get power ups and upgrades to help you defend your yard, but the rest is up to you.

Chase them away or you'll find yourself dealing with Too Many Cats!
